Brown Rice

DESCRIPTION

Ingredients
  • 1 cup long-grain brown rice
  • 1 3/4 cups water
  • optional: 1/4 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water for 10 seconds
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine the rice and water (and salt, if using)
  3. Cover with the lide and turn the heat to medium-high and bring the rice to a boil
  4. Once at a boil, set timer for 35 minutes and reduce heat to medium-low
  5. Try not to remove the lid and keep watch to make sure it does not boil over
  6. When the timer goes off, remove the lid and check for crater-like steam holes in the surface of the rice
  7. Turn off the heat and let rice sit with lid on for 10 minutes
  8. Use a fork to fluff before serving
