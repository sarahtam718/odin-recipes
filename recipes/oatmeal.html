Oatmeal

DESCRIPTION

Ingredients
  • 1 or 1/2 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds or ground flax
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup plant milk
  • optional mix-ins: vanilla protein powder, nuts, seeds, nut butter, chopped fruit

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to medium saucepan
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring and mashing banana
  3. Cook 8-10 minutes until thick and creamy
  4. Remove from heat and let thicken for a couple minutes
  5. Add toppings or more plant milk for creaminess and enjoy
