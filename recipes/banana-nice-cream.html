Banana nIce Cream

DESCRIPTION

Ingredients
  • frozen bananas
  • milk of choice, if needed
  • optional mix-ins: vanilla extract, cocount milk, peanut butter, strawberries

Steps

  1. Blend frozen bananas in a blender or food processor until the right texture is achieved
  2. Add milk a little at a time to create a smoother texture or help the appliance blend the bananas
  3. Serve immediately or freeze for 30 minutes for a firmer texture

Source: Chocolate Covered Katie
